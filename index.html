Indications

Inflammatory joint pathology, avascular necrosis, femoral neck version angle analysis, tumors, degenerative disease, etc.

Previous preparation

Remove metallic objects from abdomen and pelvis.

Patient position

Dorsal decubitus, arms up, feet internal rotation.

Anatomical reference

Iliac crests to lesser trochanters incl. acetabulum and sacroiliac joints.

WITHOUT CONTRAST

Indications

Head trauma, headaches, stroke, subarachnoid hemorrhage, suspected hydrocephalus, abnormal valve function, loss of consciousness, motility/sensory loss.

Previous preparation

Remove all metallic objects from the patient's head, face and neck.

Patient position

Dorsal decubitus, chin close to chest, head toward gantry, use foam supports for immobilization.
